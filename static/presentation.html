How It Works

Four steps from napkin sketch to running application.

💡

1. Propose

Any employee submits an idea — a process improvement, a tool, a workflow optimization. No technical knowledge required. Just describe the problem and your vision for the solution.

🗳️

2. Vote & Refine

The community votes on ideas. Once the threshold is reached, an AI facilitator guides business users through a structured conversation to build a complete specification — in plain language.

🏗️

3. Autonomous Build

A 14-phase AI build pipeline transforms the specification into a fully working web application — complete with database, backend, UI, tests, and deployment. No human developer needed.

🚀

4. Deploy & Use

The MVP is instantly accessible to the team. Users can interact, test, and provide feedback. Iteration and rebuilds are one click away.
